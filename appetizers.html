Appetizer Recipes

1. Caprese Skewers

Ingredients:

  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Fresh mozzarella balls
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper
  • Skewers or toothpicks

Steps:

  1. Thread one cherry tomato, one basil leaf, and one mozzarella ball onto each skewer.
  2. Drizzle with balsamic glaze.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

2. Spinach Artichoke Dip

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chopped spinach (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 cup chopped artichoke hearts
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Salt, pepper, and garlic powder to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl until well combined.
  3. Transfer to a baking dish and bake for 20 minutes or until bubbly and golden.
  4. Serve warm with chips, crackers, or bread.
