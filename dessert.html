Dessert Recipes

1. Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 4 oz bittersweet chocolate
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • Pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Grease ramekins.
  2. Melt butter and chocolate together until smooth.
  3. Whisk eggs, yolks, and sugar until thick.
  4. Fold in chocolate mixture, then flour and salt.
  5. Pour into ramekins and bake for 12 minutes.
  6. Invert onto plates and serve immediately.

2. Classic Tiramisu

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Ladyfinger biscuits
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Steps:

  1. Whisk egg yolks and sugar until pale and thick.
  2. Fold in mascarpone and vanilla.
  3. Whip cream until stiff peaks form, then fold into mixture.
  4. Dip ladyfingers in coffee and layer in dish.
  5. Spread mascarpone mixture over, repeat layers.
  6. Chill for 4 hours, dust with cocoa before serving.
