Dessert Recipes

1. Chocolate Mug Cake

Ingredients:

  • 4 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Mix all dry ingredients in a microwave-safe mug.
  2. Add milk and oil, stirring until smooth.
  3. Microwave for 1 minute 30 seconds or until cooked through.
  4. Let cool slightly before eating.

2. Fruit Parfait

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 cup mixed fruits (berries, banana, etc.)
  • ¼ cup granola
  • 1 tbsp honey (optional)

Steps:

  1. Layer yogurt, fruits, and granola in a glass.
  2. Drizzle honey on top if desired.
  3. Serve chilled and enjoy!
